What is VPN and why do you need it?

VPN (Virtual Private Network) is a technology that creates a secure connection between your device and the internet. VPN provides:

  • Anonymity on the internet
  • Protection from surveillance and hackers
  • Bypassing geographical restrictions
  • Security when using public Wi-Fi

How does EPIC VPN work?

EPIC VPN works as follows:

  1. Your traffic is encrypted on the device
  2. Encrypted data is sent to our servers
  3. The server decrypts the data and sends it to the internet
  4. Responses return through the same secure channel

Why shouldn't you use free VPNs?

Using free VPNs can be dangerous. Free VPN providers need to make money, so they often do so by tracking and selling your data, displaying intrusive ads, and using your devices' computing power.
